PhD candidate at FGV/EAESP with international experiences at Victoria University of Wellington and University of Victoria. My research focuses on critical management studies, decoloniality, racial relations, and organizational practices within quilombola and Indigenous communities. I have over 16 years of experience in public administration and a strong commitment to social and educational issues.

Research

  • Racial relations in organizations
  • Decoloniality and Southern epistemologies
  • Management in quilombola and Indigenous communities
  • Non-conventional organizations and critiques of mainstream management

  • CEFET-MG: Courses on Diversity Management, Organizational Psychology
  • USP/ESALQ: Advisor for MBA theses in People Management and Sales
